About

This is the website for CS 499 Team #3. Check out the Bio's page for team member information. Our client is Mr. Peter Williams from North Oldham High School. Our project is to build a system for visually impaired and blind people to shoot basketball. The home page is a feed of log entries that will track progress. Each team member will write at least one log entry per week. To view each person's log entries, go to their respective page.
